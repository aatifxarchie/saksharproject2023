WHAT IS SAKSHAR?

Sakshar is a game based learninng methodology developed at Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur. It allows kids to learn new words and play around by selecting images, listening to questions and responding through speech. It also tracks the player's analytics and enables the instructors to develop a customised learning path for the player. Currently, the game has three modules each spanning over 4 to 6 slides covering the letters - 'S', 'A' and 'T' of the English alphabet. It is being tested at Kendriya Vidyalaya, IIT Jodhpur.
